Mission!

As a God-centered company, McBride PET Corporation aims to continuously provide diversified and high quality plastic packaging for a wide-range of consumer products through innovation.

  • It aims to be the leader in the country’s plastic packaging industry by dominating the market share while maintaining efficiency in its business processes without harming the environment.
  • It values loyalty through services that results in good customer relations and satisfaction.
  • It takes pride in creating and providing jobs for Filipino citizens, in contributing to the evolution of the country’s packaging industry.
  • It commits to perform business functions with utmost honesty and integrity.

Vision!

McBride PET Corporation is a God-centered company that provides high quality plastic packaging in the ASEAN region in terms of quality, diversity of product, sales, recognition and customer satisfaction
