About Us!

Company Overview

In 2000, young and dynamic entrepreneurs saw the need to develop and create the leading manufacturer of P.E.T. bottles in the Philippines. Eventually in 2001 a lot with an area of 4.5 hectares was purchased to accommodate the plant and warehouse with all its facilities was realized. The main aim is to provide a steady supply of high quality P.E.T. (Polyethylene Terephthalate) products as well as best service to leading companies especially those engaged in condiments and beverages, cooking oil, bottled water and other chemical / pharmaceutical ventures.

Our employees

McBride is composed of employees who are supportive, strong-willed, trustworthy, and open to diversity. Trained to have a winning attitude and unwavering resolution to be the best in the field and committed to give quality work and to broaden their limits through personal and professional growth uphold by the principle of integrity, transparency, and credibility in all matters.
