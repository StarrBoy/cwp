DOCUMENT CONTROLLER

Job Summary

  • In charge of the monitoring and safekeeping of all Quality Management System Related Documents of the organization.
  • Implements the Document and record control procedure of the organization.

Statement of Duties

  • Responsible for the issuance of pertinent QMS related documents to the operations;
  • Responsible for the retrieval of obsolete records from relevant members of the organization;
  • Administers procedures on Document and Data Control and Control of Quality Records established by the organization;
  • Maintains and updates files and records relevant to the administration of the Quality Management Systems;
  • Assists the QMR in maintaining records of Management Review, relevant records of review inputs and outputs;
  • Assists the Lead Auditor in the maintenance of the Internal Audit Records
  • Performs such other duties from time to time be assigned by the QMR and/or Lead Auditor and/or the General Manager.
  • Reports directly to QMR.

Qualifications:

  • Experienced as Document Controller or Certification/Seminars about being an effective Document Controller
  • BSBA or any related courses
  • Interested to be assigned in our Kaybiga, Plant

PRODUCTION WORKER

  • COE from previous experiences
