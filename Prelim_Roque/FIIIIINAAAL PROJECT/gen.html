Message from our General Manager


Vic Pati-on, General Manager

Dear Customers and Partners,

Mcbride Corporation is a leading manufacturing company that provides plastic bottles to various clients all over the Philippines. In 2001, we started with one plant and a handful employees. As we grow, we continue to provide jobs to our fellow citizens in 4 different plants located in Metro Manila, Laguna, Bulacan and Pangasinan. We are also dedicated towards satisfying our customer needs and honoring commitments that we have made to them.

As part of the growing Plastics Industry in the Philippines, we see to it that we shared our own ways in preserving and protecting our environment through our various Corporate Social Responsibility programs. We, at Mcbride encourage all our employees and stakeholders to be part in saving our environment and proactively contribute towards nation-building.

_

Vic Pati-on General Manager
